The Manifesto

Privacy is not secrecy — it is the power to choose what the world knows about you. In an era of mass surveillance, unencrypted data streams, and corporate overreach, the right to digital autonomy is more urgent than ever.

We believe in encrypted by default, decentralized identity, and open-source security tooling. The internet was built as a free forum for ideas, not as a panopticon. Every individual deserves a private space, free from unwarranted monitoring.

"The only secure system is one that is fully transparent about its vulnerabilities — and actively works to fix them." — Cyber Vault Creed
